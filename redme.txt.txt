An interactive Excel-based dashboard analyzing 230k+ Spotify tracks using pivot tables, KPIs, slicers, and visualizations.


Key Insights:

Pop is the most popular genre (~66.5)
Electronic, Hip-Hop, and Rock have the highest energy levels
Danceability has a strong positive correlation with popularity
Low-danceability songs (0.0–0.2) perform the worst
Classical and Opera have the lowest energy values
Indie and Children’s Music show surprisingly high popularity


Dashboard Features:

KPI Tiles:
 Total Tracks
 Avg Popularity
 Avg Tempo
 Top Genre

Genre Popularity Chart
Genre Energy Chart
Danceability vs Popularity Line Chart
Interactive slicer (Genre)
Organized pivot tables on a separate sheet
